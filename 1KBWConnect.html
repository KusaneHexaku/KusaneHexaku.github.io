On the Subject of Only Connect

These fiendish cards are trying to trick our teams...

What Makes A Valid Connection?

- Something factual, and reasonably concrete
- May be thematic or qualitative

- For qualitative connections, it can be something that is true about a lot of things, but NOT a universal given.
e.g. "They all contain an animal facing left", "The point numbers on all of them are prime", "All of them requires you to flip a coin"
- For thematic connections, it should be something that at least two other players at the table understands and can agree upon.
e.g. "StephenVlogs titles", "References to the Ocarina of Time let's play", ""Things Stephen have hidden at the back of his closet", "Nintendo Games"

- It should be a little less obvious while not being overly esoteric, preferably leaning toward the latter.
e.g. "People on this channel" is too obvious, while "Things Stephen tried to eat while getting interrupted by Mal on the Breakfast Stream" is way too obtuse.
- Connections are NOT reserved only to things related to the channel. As long as it is a decent connection and the table agrees with you, then anything is fair game.
e.g. "Rick and Morty characters", "They all reference Superbowl", "They all have cheese related puns"

- Optional : Watch a couple episodes of Only Connect. You'll get a feel for how it's suppose to work.
